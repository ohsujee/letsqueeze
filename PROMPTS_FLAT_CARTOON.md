## STYLE MASTER — Duolingo-Inspired Flat

```
Flat cartoon illustration inspired by Duolingo's character design system.
Solid color fills only — NO gradients, NO blur, NO glow, NO neon,
NO photorealistic lighting, NO volumetric effects.

CHARACTER DESIGN (critical):
- Big expressive heads (1/3 to 1/2 of total body height)
- Each character has UNIQUE body proportions — some tall and thin,
  some short and wide, some round, some lanky. Diversity of body shapes
  is key to personality and avoids generic/childish look.
- Eyes: large white ovals with big black pupils. Pupils placed
  expressively (looking sideways, up, squinting). Thick eyebrows
  for emotion (raised = surprise, angled down = determination, one up = sass).
- Mouths: simple but varied — wide open smile, smirk, O-shape, tongue out.
  NOT the same smile on every character.
- NO nose in most cases. Nose only if it defines the character.
- Hair/accessories are the PRIMARY identity markers — distinct hairstyles,
  hats, glasses, headphones, scarves. Every character instantly recognizable.
- Hands are simplified mittens/blobs with thumb. Clear gesture intent.
- Arms and legs are simple tubular shapes, bendy, rubbery feel.
- Clothing in bold solid colors — each character has their own color scheme.

EXPRESSIONS & POSES:
- Characters have ATTITUDE and PERSONALITY, not just happiness.
  Sass, determination, mischief, competitive fire, smug confidence.
- Poses are highly dynamic — strong diagonals, leaning bodies, arms reaching,
  bodies overlapping each other. Squash & stretch energy.
- Characters INTERACT — overlapping, bumping into each other, reacting
  to each other. NOT standing in a neat row.

COLOR RULES:
- Solid fills only. Every surface = ONE flat color.
- Shadows: single darker shade on select surfaces (not everywhere),
  hard-edged, placed to suggest volume. Minimal — not on every shape.
- NO outlines on characters — shapes defined by color contrast alone.
- Palette: vibrant but NOT primary. Muted pastels + bold saturated accents.
  Think: pink, moutarde yellow, teal, coral, lavender — NOT red/blue/yellow.
- Background: deep solid color matching the game's theme color.

COMPOSITION:
- Characters are the HEROES — they fill most of the frame.
- Overlapping and layering between characters creates depth.
- Dynamic diagonal energy — the group leans, tilts, reaches.
- Props (buzzers, microphones, cards) are oversized and expressive.
- Minimal environment — background is mostly solid color.
  The focus is 90% on the characters and their energy.

Reference: Duolingo blog illustrations, Duolingo character cast (Lily, Bea,
Oscar, Zari, Lin, Junior, Eddy), Duolingo World campaign art.

STRICT RULES:
- NO gradients or color transitions
- NO glow, bloom, lens flare, or light effects
- NO blur of any kind
- NO text, words, letters, or UI elements
- NO black outlines on characters (NOT comic book style)
- NO realistic proportions — stylized cartoon with personality
- NO identical body types — every character looks different
- NO generic happy faces — each character has distinct attitude
- NO detailed environments — characters ARE the scene
```
